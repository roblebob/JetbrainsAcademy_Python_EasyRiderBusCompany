Multilingual dictionary

Suppose that english, spanish and french are pre-defined lists of words in English, Spanish, and French respectively.

Imagine that you are developing a multilingual dictionary. Print out the words in three languages in one line, e.g.:

hello hola bonjour
thank you gracias merci

You can consider the aforementioned lists english, spanish and french defined and use them in your solution.



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 15 seconds
