Shopping list

Ruth goes shopping today, so she is busy making a shopping list.

She wrote down things that she would like to buy at the grocery store. All of these things are in the given shopping_list variable, it has already been created.

When sneaking a look at the fridge, she noticed that there was some milk left and changed her mind about buying a new one. It got crossed out of the list. And, finally, she added a "brownie", and it became the last item Ruth had put on the list.

Now you try to remove "milk" from the given variable shopping_list, and add "brownie" to it.

DON'T print the results.

You can add an item with list.append() and delete it with list.remove().
